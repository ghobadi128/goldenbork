Bay Area Local

Dog-Friendly Spots Near You

Local guides written by people who live here and walk these parks and trails with their own dogs.

Featured

Point Isabel Regional Shoreline, Richmond

One of the largest off-leash dog parks in the country at 23 acres. Bayfront paths, a dog wash station, and Mudpuppy's café right at the entrance. Typically less crowded on weekday mornings.

Read the Full Guide →
San Francisco

Fort Funston Off-Leash Area

Cliff-top trails and a long sandy beach. Popular with dogs who love sand. Note: erosion has closed some paths — check current conditions before going.
Oakland

Lake Merritt Dog Areas

Three designated off-leash sections around the lake. The east side near the bird sanctuary tends to be quietest.
Marin

Tennessee Valley Trail

A 3.4-mile round trip to a secluded beach. Creek crossings along the way — dogs love the water. Parking fills up by 10am on weekends.
