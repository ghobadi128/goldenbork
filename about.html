About GoldenBork

Last updated May 2025

What This Site Is

GoldenBork is a dog lifestyle resource focused on the San Francisco Bay Area. We write gear reviews, local park and trail guides, and practical information for dog owners who live here.

We're a small team of Bay Area dog owners. We started this site because we got tired of reading generic gear reviews that didn't account for apartment living, coastal weather, or the specific parks and trails we actually use. So we built the resource we wished existed.

How We Test & Write

Our process is straightforward:

  • We buy everything ourselves. No manufacturer-provided review units. This keeps our opinions independent.
  • We test in real conditions. Dog beds get used in actual apartments. Harnesses get worn on actual trails. We don't do lab tests or simulations.
  • We test for at least 4 weeks. First impressions are unreliable. We want to know how things hold up after a month of daily use.
  • We update regularly. Every article shows when it was last updated. We revisit reviews when new products come out or when our experience with existing products changes over time.
  • We say what doesn't work. If a product has problems, we describe them. If we wouldn't recommend something, we say so directly.

What We Don't Cover

We don't write about dog food or nutrition. These topics require veterinary expertise that we don't have, and the right food for your dog depends on factors (breed, age, health conditions, allergies) that vary too much for general recommendations to be useful. Talk to your vet.

We also don't cover licensing, registration, or regulatory topics. Your city or county government is the right source for that information, and it changes frequently.

Affiliate Disclosure

GoldenBork is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click a product link on our site and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Here's what this means in practice:

  • The commission we earn is the same percentage regardless of which product you buy. We have no financial incentive to recommend one product over another.
  • We choose which products to review based on what we think Bay Area dog owners would find useful, not based on which products pay higher commissions.
  • Our rankings and scores are based entirely on our testing results.
  • We clearly mark affiliate links. If a link goes to Amazon, it's an affiliate link.
  • This affiliate income is what allows us to keep the site free and to buy new products to test.

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships, we're happy to answer them.

Editorial Independence

No company has editorial input on our content. We don't accept payment for reviews. We don't accept free products in exchange for coverage. Our articles are not reviewed or approved by any brand before publication.

When we make a mistake — and we sometimes do — we correct it, note the correction, and update the article.

A Note on AI

Some content on this site is drafted or assisted by AI tools. However, every piece of information is verified by our team, and all product testing and local observations come from real, in-person experience. We use AI to help us write more efficiently, not to replace firsthand knowledge. When we say we tested something, we tested it.
